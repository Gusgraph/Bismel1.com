Hold/add/close/reconcile decisions
- hold when a broker position exists and no risk-approved management action is needed
- add when the strategy evaluation and risk layer allow a closed-candle add action
- close when the strategy evaluation and risk layer allow a closed-candle close action
- reconcile when broker position state is absent but recent local trading records still indicate prior open state

Local vs broker reconciliation behavior
- broker-synced position state remains primary for actual open-position existence
- missing broker position with recent local order or signal state records a safe reconcile outcome
- reconcile updates local summaries and linkage without exposing raw broker payloads

Trailing protection summary wording
- close outcomes are summarized as AI closed on trailing protection
- hold outcomes are summarized as AI held position

Safe user-visible/admin-visible summaries
- AI added to position
- AI held position
- AI closed on trailing protection
- AI reconciled broker state
- AI skipped management action

Hidden strategy handling
- no raw Pine or Python formulas are exposed
- proprietary strategy state remains restricted to internal payload storage only
