# Post-MVP Deferred

## Deferred To Post-MVP

- Real broker provider integrations and sync jobs
- Real API/license verification against external systems
- Multi-role admin/customer authorization hardening
- Production-grade notifications, retries, and audit enrichment
- Advanced reporting, charts, and historical comparisons
- E2E/browser automation
- Export/download workflows
- Bulk admin operations and operational tooling
- Full deployment hardening, observability, and error reporting

## Why Deferred

- These items increase integration and operational risk without changing the core MVP proof: local DB-backed customer/admin product surfaces with safe write flows and masked secret handling.
- The current app is strongest as a clean local MVP foundation. Expanding beyond that should happen after the current structure is intentionally stabilized.
