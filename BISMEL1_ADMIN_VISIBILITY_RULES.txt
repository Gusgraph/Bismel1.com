Admin-safe runtime summaries
- show active, stopped, blocked, broker ready, waiting for next bar close, and recent run completed using safe wording only
- keep execution, risk, and reconciliation outcomes summary-first and operational

Blocked vs ready categories
- ready requires active Bismel1 mapping, usable broker account state, and approved market-data posture
- blocked categories stay high level: strategy mapping missing, broker connection missing, credential missing, account unready, sync stale, or market data unavailable

Execution, risk, and reconciliation wording
- execution summaries describe submitted, skipped, failed, or reviewed outcomes without raw broker payloads
- risk summaries describe safe block reasons without thresholds or formulas
- reconciliation and position-manager summaries describe hold, add, close, or reconcile outcomes without internal logic detail

Hidden strategy handling
- no raw Pine logic, Python logic, formulas, or exact thresholds appear in admin visibility
- default to safe summaries, counts, timestamps, and operational categories
